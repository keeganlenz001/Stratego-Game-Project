Our Stratego Rules

Objective: Capture the openents flag

Layout: The player can move on all green tiles, but cannot move on blue tiles with the exception of the Captain unit.

Each Player will start out with 30 pieces that occupy three rows on the board. The pieces can be arranged however the players want within the first 3 rows.

Each unit has a number value that determines strength; lower # > higher power

Marshal; 1 (1x)

General; 2 (1x)

Colonel; 3 (2x)

Major; 4 (2x)

Captain; 5 (Has a boat that crosses water) (4x)

Lieutenant; 6 (Can attack from 2 spaces away) (4x)

Sergeant; 7 (Decreases the level of Marshal by 1 on attack) (4x)

Miner; 8 (5x)

Scout; 9 (Can move as much as they want) (6x)

Flag: the target of the game; completely stationary and can’t perform actions. (1x)

Encounters can be triggered by a unit attacking another. The units’ numbers are revealed to each other and the lower number wins. If their numbers are equal, both units die. If the unit who started the encounter wins, they take the loser’s place. If the attacked unit wins, they stay in the same place and don’t move.
