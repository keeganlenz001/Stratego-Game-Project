Stratego by Spencer Folsom and Keegan Lenz

Objective: Capture the openents flag

Layout: The player can move on all green tiles, but cannot move on blue tiles with the exception of the Captain unit.

Players will take turns moving pieces. Red always goes first.

Each Player will start out with 30 pieces that occupy three rows on the board. The pieces can be arranged however the players want within the first 3 rows of the board.

Each unit has a number value that determines strength; lower # = higher power

The (x) signifies the number of pieces, while the number after the semi-colon determines the attack power.

Marshal; 1 (1x)

General; 2 (1x)

Colonel; 3 (2x)

Major; 4 (2x)

Captain; 5 (Has a boat that crosses water) (4x)

Lieutenant; 6 (Can move 2 spaces and attack on same turn) (4x)

Sergeant; 7 (Decreases the level of Marshal by 1 on attack. This porcess can only be done once.) (4x)

Miner; 8 (5x)

Scout; 9 (Can move as much as they want) (6x)

Flag: the target of the game; completely stationary and canâ€™t perform actions. (1x)

Encounters can be triggered by a unit attacking another. The lower number between the units wins. If their numbers are equal, both units die. You cannot attack and move in the same turn. If your unit dies, place the unit in the large box according to your color.
